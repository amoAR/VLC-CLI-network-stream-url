These are options of playlist --> generate - play - reset  :
Path: executionLocation\Database\playlist.xspf



...\...\myList.txt
	Generate the playlist
	Give the path of the list file containing URLs

	*URL lines must start with HTTP or https
	*There must be a video format at the end of the URLs

	Example myList.txt:
	episode 1
	https://netflix.com/the_name_S01E01_quality.mkv

	episode 2
	http://Pahe.in/name_S01E02.mp4


play playlist, 1
	Play the playlist
	Open your last created playlist


reset playlist, 2
	Clear the playlist
